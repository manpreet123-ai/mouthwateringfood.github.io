Low Calorie Mexican Salad Indian Style

Prep Time: 15 mins
Resting Toime: 2 hours

Who says when you are Dieting you have to have unappealing boring food. Here is a very easy to make yet Tasty and Filling salad recipe. When we are dieting or in weight managemet mode finding delicious low calorie recipes is difficult. It is important that the food we eat should not only be delicious but should be visually appealing too. And this Mexican Red Beans Salad is not only healthy but super sumptious too.

For Begginers
How to Make Low Calorie Salad Indian Style :
(1)To Prepare Rajma Layer:
(2)Wash and soak the rajma overnight. In the morning drain the water and pressure cook them in 1 cup water for 2-4 whistles and later for 10 minutes on low flame.
(3)Let the pressure release on its own. Drain the water, mash them a little and then keep it aside
(4)In a pan heat the oil and add the minced garlic. When slightly pink add the chopped onion and green chilly. Let it stir fry till little pinkish in color. Now add the tomato puree, salt, oregano, tomato ketchup and red chilly..
(5)Let it simmer till the puree thickens with ocassional stir.
(6)Now add the mashed kidney beans.And mix well. Check for seasoning
(7)Once thick switch off the flame.
(8)Mix all the ingredients of the salad dressing and keep aside.
(9)Put all the chopped vegetables in a bowl and just before serving pour the dressing over the salad.
(10)To Prepare Curd Layer:
(11)Hang the curd in a muslin cloth or in a thin strainer till all the water drain out.
(12)Then put it in a bowl and whisk smooth and then add salt, minced garlic and then keep it aside for some time.
(13)To Assemble The Salad Platter:
(14)When it time to serve, put the salad on a platter and then add the rajma mashed mixture on it and then finally pour the curd dressing.
(15)Garnish it with red chilly flakes and chopped green coriander and few olives.
(16)TIPS/ SUGGESTIONS ON MEXICAN BEANS SALAD:
(17)You can add fresh pineapple pieces to add more zing to the salad.
(18)In case you are preparing for your family members and kids you can add grated amul cheese and serve it with Nachos.)
(19)You can stuff this salad in a wrap and make a delicious and complete meal for kids. In case you are a vegetarian you can add grilled chicken to the salad.
(20)Also to increase the protein simply add some cubed paneer pieces. To make the Curd Dip more creamier you can add 1 tblsp of fresh cream.
